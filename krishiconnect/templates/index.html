How It Works

An intuitive workflow for farmers and customers to trade, manage orders, and stay connected.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

Register: Create profile in E-Farmer's Hut.
Upload: Add product details and manage inventory.
Orders: Receive and accept order notifications.
Deliver: Fulfill the order to the customer.

🛒 For Customers

Register: Create a customer profile.
Shop: Browse products, compare, and add to cart.
Payment: Pay Online or Cash on Delivery (COD).
Feedback: Rate the delivery and the app.
